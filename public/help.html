Click on 'Add-ons' and then hover over 'Wordcount Logger'. From the menu that appears, click on 'Start'.

Wordcount Logger will automatically start logging your wordcount for the session.

Log in to ThinkPlanWrite.com to store logs and view historic stats about your writing logs.

If you don't already have an account, you can register with ThinkPlanWrite.com and then store your writing log.
